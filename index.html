Who benefits?

Server Owners

Have a great server but no one seems to be able to find it? Us too! Your server will be on our personal server list which ranks servers with more than just votes. We log many aspects of your server, including your plugins and custom plugins, chat rate, players online, community feedback, player turnover rate, server lag, and pvp rates. We want your server to get the recognition it deserves.

Sign Up

Developers

Easily expose your new minigames and gamemodes to the public! Our Arcade is the perfect way for you to get recognition. Specifically, if you hook into our Game API, we will link players to your portfolio before and after the game starts. With the API, you will be able to add your game to our Arcade seamlessly, allowing players to find your server. Also, you can use our forums to offer your services as a developer. It's a win-win situation.
